About Qube Catalyst

I’m Pearl Oguchi, founder of Qube Catalyst. I help founders turn messy app builds into launch-ready products that actually work.

I’ve spent over 20 years working on product quality and release in software, including 8 years at Meta. My work there involved being embedded across the end-to-end development process – from early ideas to launch – seeing exactly where things break and how misaligned teams create chaos.

Now I focus on non-technical founders and lean teams who are building serious products without a dedicated technical lead. You shouldn’t have to become an engineer to get a product that behaves well and makes sense in the real world.

How I think about product confidence

Product confidence is about more than “does it load?”. It’s whether the experience your users have actually matches the promise you’re making in your pitch deck, your marketing, and your investor conversations.

My work blends:

  • Technical clarity – making sense of what your team or agency has actually built.
  • Issue triage – separating “annoying” from “launch-blocking” problems.
  • Journey and design thinking – checking whether flows are usable, not just “implemented.”
  • Go-to-market awareness – making sure the product experience supports your growth story.

Services

Micro Confidence Review

A £300–£400 micro service focused on your 3–4 most critical flows. You’ll get:

  • Clear descriptions of what’s breaking or confusing for users.
  • Severity-based priorities tied to impact on conversion and trust.
  • A same-day action plan that your team or agency can act on.

Launch Compass – Product Readiness Tool

A self-serve web tool for non-technical founders who have been left to “check it themselves”. Launch Compass turns your answers into a structured readiness view so you can have grown-up conversations with your build team or agency.
