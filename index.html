The Real Problem Founders Face

When you're a non-technical founder building an app with an agency, freelance developer or AI tools, you're operating half blind. You can’t tell if decisions make sense, if delays are reasonable, or if the build is launch-ready or just demo-ready. You’ve invested real app development budget, but you’re still guessing.

This isn’t a coding problem. It’s an oversight and product direction problem; an app roadmap and confidence problem.

Q³ acts as your second brain and launch partner, spotting early issues like scope creep or unclear user flows, and guiding you from “work in progress” to a product that’s genuinely ready to launch.

How Qube Catalyst helps

Q³ supports non-technical founders from app idea to launch by combining technical oversight, product strategy and practical leadership, so you can avoid costly app development mistakes and launch with confidence instead of hope.

Fractional Launch Partner

For teams beyond first launch who need ongoing support from idea to go-to-market. I sit alongside you as a product confidence partner – helping you shape your app roadmap, make better trade-offs between features and budget, and keep each release aligned with what your users and revenue actually need.
