What happens next

1

You'll hear from Pearl within one working day

Pearl will reach out by email to introduce herself properly and confirm your availability for the working sessions.

2

We'll schedule your sessions

Sessions are scheduled within your 5-day Sprint window at times that work for you.

3

Share what you have

Before we start, I will ask you to share any relevant documents: scope, quotes, backlog, contracts, or plans. Don't worry if it's incomplete or messy; that's normal and exactly what the Sprint is here to work through.

4

You'll receive your Clarity Pack

By the end of day 5 you'll have a written pack covering your build summary, MVP boundary, risk list, responsibilities, and recommendations... in plain English.

Questions in the meantime? Email Pearl directly at pearlo@qubecatalyst.com
