What the Decision Clarity Consultation is.

The DCC is a 90-minute session with me; and I have absolutely no stake in what you decide.

Not your agency.

Not a project manager.

Not someone trying to win your ongoing work.

Someone whose only job is to give you the honest picture, in plain language, before you make a decision that's difficult to reverse.

Who it's perfect for.

This session is for founders, MDs, and business owners who have commissioned software and are expected to approve, sign off, or launch something they didn't build and can't fully verify.
