Introducing VerAIQ: The Product Readiness Tool for Founders Building Without a Tech Lead

Most non-technical founders are flying blind when it comes to launch readiness. You get a demo. It looks fine. Your developer says, “We’re ready.”

But you can’t see what’s beneath the surface. You don’t know if the flows break under real use. You can’t tell whether you’re looking at a solid build or a house of cards. That gap, that uncertainty, is where launches go wrong.

The Real Problem VerAIQ Solves

When you don’t have a technical co-founder, you usually end up doing one of two things:

  • You trust the team and hope for the best: Risky. If something goes wrong at launch, users won’t care whose fault it was.
  • You delay the launch because something feels off: Also expensive. Every week you wait is another week the market moves on.

Neither path gives you confidence. Both force you to guess. VeraiQ exists to eliminate the guesswork.

What VerAIQ Actually Does

VerAIQ guides you, step by step, through a clear, plain-English process that helps you surface the real truth about your product:

  • What users are supposed to do (your core journeys)
  • What absolutely must work on day one
  • Which flows carry the most risk
  • Where unknowns or gaps could embarrass you at launch

Then it turns all of that into a structured Product Readiness View; a simple, visual snapshot of whether you’re genuinely ready to launch, or heading for a round of firefighting. No technical experience needed. No developer translation required. Just clarity.

Why It Was Built

I’ve spent years helping founders who were brilliant at their ideas and market — but completely lost when assessing whether their product was “good enough” to go live.

The questions were always the same:

“Is this timeline normal?”

“Why does every build suddenly explode right before launch?”

“My developer says it’s done, but… is it really?”

You shouldn’t need a fractional CTO just to understand your own product. VerAIQ gives you a starting point. A way to verify before you trust. A way to launch with your eyes open, not crossed fingers.

Who is VerAIQ For

VerAIQ is built for founders who:

  • Are working with freelancers, agencies or offshore dev teams
  • Don’t have a technical co-founder
  • Need an objective way to assess launch readiness
  • Want to avoid the classic “launch → crash → scramble → patch” cycle
  • Prefer clarity over chaos
If that’s you, VeraiQ gives you structure and certainty in a space that usually has neither.
