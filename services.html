How I diagnose

I do not start with user calls. I start with your product and your data. Four lenses, in this order.

1

Product walkthrough

I use your product the way your users do, end to end, on real devices. Not your power users, not your team. The quiet majority who try it once, shrug, and leave. I write down every place I hesitate, get confused, or give up.

2

Pattern check

I compare what I see against patterns from 22 years of shipping products and a 52,000 member operator network. It is not a checklist. It is pattern recognition about what tends to hold and what tends to break when you grow.

3

Engagement and funnel data

I look at where users drop, where they stall, and where the metric you report to the board is different from the metric that really predicts retention. I read your data the way an operator reads it, not the way a slide reads it.

4

User validation, when needed

When the first three lenses leave a question I cannot answer and only the user can close it, I run focused validation. It is not a default deliverable. It only happens when the diagnosis truly needs it.
